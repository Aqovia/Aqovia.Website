General data protection act (GDPR) is real

Businesses in the UK have started to become GDPR compliant to avoid losing up to €20 million or 4% of their organisation's global annual turnover.

You will need to comply with the GDPR if your organisation stores and/or processes personal data of EU citizens.

Ignoring GDPR is a mistake

Regardless of whether you are a multi-national, large or small organisation non-compliance with the GDPR leads to hefty penalties with serious financial consequence. Every organisation across the value chain will need to be GDPR compliant, therefore supplier and partner selection process will now include assessing whether the enterprise is GDPR compliant.
